# Code of Conduct

We are committed to creating a welcoming and inclusive environment.  

## Our Standards
- Be respectful and considerate.  
- Provide constructive feedback.  
- Use inclusive and appropriate language.  

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment or discrimination of any kind.  
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments.  
- Any behavior that makes others uncomfortable.  

## Enforcement
Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in removal from the community.
